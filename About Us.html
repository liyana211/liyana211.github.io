About Us

The leading online travel platform, dedicated to the modern Muslim traveller.

Tripfez Travel was founded by Faeez Fadhlillah in Malaysia in 2013 to serve the rise of the global Muslim market — which is expected to represent 25% of global travel expenditure by 2020.

Our vision is to help Muslim travellers to explore the world in a faith-compliant manner. Our online travel platform focuses on online bookings of halal-friendly travel packages and Umrah. As a full-service agency, we handle flights, hotels, ground arrangements, visa applications and insurance.

Our Story

Mission

Making Muslim travel ridiculously easy.

Here's an idea!

Being Muslim and travelling at the same time can add additional responsibilities. From making sure your solah is on time, until the meals you eat are ensured halal (that’s the tip of the iceberg), we need to take into consideration what makes us Muslim while roaming the vast, vast world.  

Now, imagine getting help from fellow Muslim travellers. Making sure from the time you reach your dream destination until you return home, your Muslim values are not compromised. With Tripfez, you are free to travel the world while keeping your Deen in check.  

We found out the hard way how difficult it was to travel while keeping your Muslim values in check. After going through the struggles of creating Muslim-centred itineraries, the journey inspired us to create Tripfez in order to help other Muslim travellers locally and globally.

Read more about our CEO and Co-founder, Faeez.

So, who is Tripfez?

Tripfez is now one of the fast-growing B2C online travel startup in Asia, dedicated to the rise of the modern Muslim traveller. 

In 2018, Tripfez merged with the Dubai-based HolidayMe creating an even larger platform for us to grow and make an impact. We are focussed completely on the Malaysian consumer at Tripfez and have been featured on the BBC, CNBC, Forbes, The Star and many more media outlets.

We are proud to have a working environment that sets our employees up for success with active coaching from the senior team, as well as some cool perks you would expect from a startup! We aim to create a place where people can thrive and be trusted to make an impact and ultimately to help us serve our customers better. Find out how to join us!
